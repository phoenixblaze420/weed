Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our revolutionary cannabis platform

How does the dropshipping model work?

Our platform connects you directly with verified suppliers. When a customer places an order through your store, the order is automatically forwarded to the relevant supplier who handles packaging and shipping directly to your customer. You never have to manage inventory, handle products, or deal with logistics.

What equipment do I need to use the AR/VR design tools?

Our AR tools work on most modern smartphones and tablets with AR capabilities. For the full VR experience, we recommend a VR headset such as Meta Quest, HTC Vive, or similar. However, we also offer a desktop version that works on standard computers without specialized hardware.

How does the 3D printing network operate?

When you design or select a 3D model in our system, we match you with the closest 3D printing service in our global network. They produce the item and ship it directly to you. We've vetted all providers for quality and reliability, and all designs are tested for functionality before being made available.

What makes your AI growing system different?

Our AI has been trained on millions of growing cycles across thousands of cannabis strains. It continuously learns from user data to improve recommendations. Unlike other systems that focus just on environment control, our AI provides strain-specific feeding schedules, identifies potential issues before they're visible to the human eye, and adapts to your specific growing conditions.

Is the platform legal in all areas?

Our platform operates in compliance with local regulations. We use geolocation to ensure that products and services offered through our system are legally available in your jurisdiction. Some features may be limited depending on your location's legal status regarding cannabis cultivation and consumption. Always ensure you're operating within your local laws.

    Got Questions?

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Everything you need to know about our revolutionary cannabis platform

    How does the dropshipping model work?

    Our platform connects you directly with verified suppliers. When a customer places an order through your store, the order is automatically forwarded to the relevant supplier who handles packaging and shipping directly to your customer. You never have to manage inventory, handle products, or deal with logistics.

    What equipment do I need to use the AR/VR design tools?

    Our AR tools work on most modern smartphones and tablets with AR capabilities. For the full VR experience, we recommend a VR headset such as Meta Quest, HTC Vive, or similar. However, we also offer a desktop version that works on standard computers without specialized hardware.

    How does the 3D printing network operate?

    When you design or select a 3D model in our system, we match you with the closest 3D printing service in our global network. They produce the item and ship it directly to you. We've vetted all providers for quality and reliability, and all designs are tested for functionality before being made available.

    What makes your AI growing system different?

    Our AI has been trained on millions of growing cycles across thousands of cannabis strains. It continuously learns from user data to improve recommendations. Unlike other systems that focus just on environment control, our AI provides strain-specific feeding schedules, identifies potential issues before they're visible to the human eye, and adapts to your specific growing conditions.

    Is the platform legal in all areas?

    Our platform operates in compliance with local regulations. We use geolocation to ensure that products and services offered through our system are legally available in your jurisdiction. Some features may be limited depending on your location's legal status regarding cannabis cultivation and consumption. Always ensure you're operating within your local laws.
